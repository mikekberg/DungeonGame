Stats Model


-Experience based
-Stats include:  Strength,  Agility,  Intelligence,  Constitution
-On level up, you add points to build stats

Strength - affects melee damage
Agility - affects weapon speed, magic cool down
Intelligence - affects spell damage
Constitution - affects hitpoints



Weapons and Armor

-One slot for Weapons
-One slot for Armour
-Weapons and Armour have slot(s) you can attach augments to
-Class specific augments can only be attached to certain weapon and armour types (eg. augments to increase magic can only be attached to staffs and wands)
-Other augments (2x experience, increase hit%, etc.) can be attached to any item
